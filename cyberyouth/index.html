Our Story

Cyber Youth Group was created to empower young people through unity, financial growth, and community support. We help members build financial discipline, access affordable credit, and support one another during important moments. Together, we're shaping a stronger and more connected generation.

Read more

Our Vision

To create a united and empowered community where young people can achieve financial independence, mutual support, and sustainable growth through collaboration and innovation.

Our Mission

We exist to empower members by fostering a culture of saving, offering affordable credit, promoting community welfare, and ensuring mutual support in times of need and emergencies.
